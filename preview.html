Our Story

Named with love. Cleaned with care.

Don't Worry Cleaning — DWC — isn't just a business name. It's a tribute. The initials carry deep personal meaning, honoring someone special who taught us that a clean, cared-for home is an act of love.

That spirit lives in every home we clean. When Christi started DWC, the goal was simple: bring warmth, reliability, and attention to detail to busy Twin Cities families who need a hand keeping up with life.

We're not a big corporate cleaning company. We're your neighbor. We remember your preferences, treat your home with respect, and show up when we say we will. Because that's what family does.
